  Continuous issuance renews the incentive gradient every step (each causal-set birth = one "mining"
  event). **Keep typed apart:** the issuance itself is homogeneous (more of the same — the additive
  wall); the *emergent collaboration* is the growth. Issuance is fuel, not the growth.
- **R4 (source-internal / self-generating).** Decentralization = no external mint, no completed
  oracle; legitimacy endogenous. The strongest form: **the novelty event is creating new observers**

  fork:** open-ended (new observer *types*) vs. replication (new *instances*) — the same additive
  wall at the observer level; open-endedness is the right frame and an UNSOLVED frontier.
- **R5 (recordable witness, d>0).** The ledger/poset IS the witness; each new element is a genuine
  function of its causal past (real transduction, d>0), not a relabel.
- **R6 (non-additive count, sourced coefficient).** Super-additive network value (Metcalfe-like) as
  the non-additive count; the DE count = the observer/record count coupled to expansion, closing a
  loop: Λ drives expansion → expansion accretes records → `N` grows → `Λ~1/√N` responds. Λ becomes
  the *state of a feedback loop*, not a constant. **Magnitude crux (the load-bearing lever):** the
  controller must be **self-organized-criticality-like (set-point-free)**, NOT a PID / difficulty-
  adjustment (which regulates to an *imported* target — Bitcoin's 10-min block time is a design

- **R7 (external sign).** The DE **sign** stays the one external bit (σ); in the causal-set picture

"What is ingested from one state to the next could be from the previous state" + Hashgraph's
**gossip-about-gossip** (each event carries the hashes of its parent events — the history of how

existing causal set (its past = self-parent + other-parent), transition probabilities are functions
of the *existing* causet (source-internal, R4), and the growth is **discretely covariant** —
birth-order-independent, no preferred foliation (this is the SAME covariance the running
covariant-finality swing needs). Fit is remarkably good for R3 (each birth = continuous issuance),
R4 (internal + covariant), R5 (d>0), R7 (`±√N` sign external).

**Sorkin appears three times and they converge:** causal sets (the `Λ~1/√N` DE amplitude),
