# Self-authoring Dirac — build+kill synthesis (trunk attempt #2)

The repaired trunk: a self-authoring gossip-about-gossip Dirac (growth = gossip-about-gossip; D on the
order complex; spectrum recorded back). Independent build (PARTIAL) + adversarial kill (ABSORBED). They
**agree on the mechanism**; they split on the grade, and the split is exactly the source-is-observer
question. The honest result splits cleanly by leg.

## The OBSERVER / BECOMING leg — genuinely repaired, but its L2 is external
- **L1 self-encoding is installed (real, decisive).** The consulted admissibility predicate IS a
  self-encoded knowledge set on **0.68** of steps vs **0.00** for the blind (spectrum-not-recorded-back)
  control — the feedback makes the difference. The naive flow (attempt #1) had "no inside at all"; this
  one has an inside. That is a genuine advance.
- **But the diagonal needs L2 — a fixpoint-free involution — and a monotone self-authoring loop cannot
  supply it.** Gossip forwarding only *adds*, never *flips*; by **Kleene's recursion theorem** a monotone
  self-referential map has a fixpoint = a self-consistent causet = a **quine** = disclosure. So
  self-referential-*looking* ≠ self-referentially-*undecidable*. The diagonal fires only with a genuine
  negation, and **DU's only fixpoint-free involution is the external Krein σ** (Gödel-independent, R7).
  Compose σ and both-true becoming fires (third-person computable, first-person underivable) — but the
  orienting datum is then **external**.
- **Reading:** this is *source-is-observer confirmed on the construction* — "the inside-ness of the
  observer forces the outside-ness of the minted datum," now a theorem: a *fully* source-internal mint is
  a quine (disclosure); genuine becoming *requires* one external involution, which is σ. σ gains a role
  it never had — not just "the external sign" (R7) but **the L2 that fires the becoming-diagonal.** The
  bottleneck (can't internalize the orienting bit) is theorem-shaped, not a missing construction.
- **Honest open caveat (build, pre-registered):** even with σ, the feedback rule is fixed/deterministic,
  so this is *becoming-first-person compatible with third-person disclosure* (the reframe's target), NOT
  third-person incomputability. Whether "first-person underivability under a *fixed* rule" is *genuine*
  becoming or a sophisticated disclosure **stays open** — a foundational / ai-epistemology question, not
  settled here.

## The DE-VALUE legs — still NULL (unchanged by the self-authoring loop)
- **Non-additive W1: NULL, but the wall is LIFTED not populated.** Putting D on the order/reference
  complex genuinely **escapes the free-H₁ theorem** (H₂≠0 in every run; the 1-skeleton has H²=0) — a real
  structural gain over attempt #1's *provable* additive wall. **But 0/24 torsion, 0/24 nonzero cup
  product**: the gossip reference complex is a union of small simplices glued on shared faces →
  generically free (⊕Z, Θ_type=1). Torsion needs degree-≥2 attaching maps the dynamics never generate.
  So the *arena* for non-additivity now exists, but the dynamics don't *populate* it → the DE
  non-additive count is still not sourced.
- **Transmutation: NULL.** Gap = imported cutoff (gap/w constant); the dimensionless coupling does not run
  (self-authoring and blind both ≈0.18); no β-function, no density-forgetting fixed point. Scale still
  unsourced.

## Net — the trunk splits into two separable problems
1. **Observer/becoming:** *constructible* now (self-encoding installed), but genuine becoming **requires
   the external σ** (Kleene → source-is-observer), and whether fixed-rule first-person-underivability is
   *genuine* becoming is open. Advance, with two honest caveats.
2. **DE value (the anchor's O(1) coefficient):** **untouched** by the self-authoring loop — non-additive
   count (torsion unpopulated) and transmutation (scale = cutoff) both still NULL. This is the R6 /
   SOC-vs-set-point / dimensional-transmutation problem, and the self-authoring loop did **not** help it.

The two are *separable*: the elephant now has an inside, but (a) its becoming rides on an external
heart-bit (σ), and (b) it still cannot source its own dark-energy value. Attempts #1 and #2 together
localize the remaining work precisely: **populate torsion** (degree-≥2 attaching maps / a non-free
mechanism) and **source the scale** (a genuine β-function) — neither of which the observer/becoming leg
supplies.
