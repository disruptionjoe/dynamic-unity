# Stratified finality

Distributed-consensus systems (Bitcoin, DAGs, Hashgraph's gossip-about-gossip, Rideout–Sorkin CSG)
expose a **responsiveness↔settlement-finality tradeoff** and, with it, that finality is **layered** —
and people routinely conflate the layers. Read into the physics, finality is a **μ-graded gradient**
(μ = TI's reversal cost = "how hard to undo" = how final), with regimes:

- **L0 — local / "final for me" / metastable.** Fast, responsive, **reversible** (low μ), and
  **observer/frame-relative.** The DAG frontier tip; a soft-confirmation. In QM: the reversible
  pointer state / Wigner's-friend-inside-the-box.
- **L1 — DAG-consensus / gossip-about-gossip / BFT.** Redundancy proliferating through the interaction
  graph; harder to undo (mid μ). In QM: quantum-Darwinism redundancy → emergent objectivity.
- **L-deep — social / economic / collective.** The observer-collective's *continued investment*
  (proof-of-work spent, value held). Highest μ, yet paradoxically the most **alive/revisable** (a
  hard-fork can undo even BFT finality). In physics: deep irreversible cosmological record-accretion
  (the roll). Never absolutely settled — a *dynamic equilibrium* maintained by ongoing participation.

## Payoff 1 — resolves our own frame-relative-vs-covariant tension
The covariant-finality result proved finality-on-J⁺ is frame-independent — yet "final for me" is
obviously observer-relative. Stratification dissolves the apparent conflict: **L0 (observer-relative,
reversible) IS the frame-dependent sliced readout the covariant-finality kill flagged as the discarded
`dR` frontier**; **L-deep (the H¹ global section) is the covariant one.** Both are real, at different
levels. Frame-dependence is a *property of the shallow layer*, not a defect — and the deep layer stays
covariant. This is exactly the "two objects, one letter" clarity our adversary register demands.

## Payoff 2 — μ finally has its job
μ (reversal cost; TI's two sources: Landauer + computational-irreducibility) was **Born-blind** (Bet
#1) — it is the wrong tool for weighting outcomes. But it is the **right tool for grading finality**:
the levels are **μ-strata**. Low μ = metastable/reversible; high μ = deep-final. μ = thermodynamic
depth (Landauer cost to reverse). Its real role was never Born; it was the finality gradient.

## Payoff 3 — PRED-DU-001 sharpens to a possible multi-knee staircase
The recoverability knee is a **μ-crossing** (metastable → deep). If finality is genuinely layered,
`V(R)`-recoverability may show **more than one knee** (L0→L1→L-deep), a *staircase*, not a single step —
a testable refinement. (Honest: if μ is a smooth gradient rather than discrete levels, the "knees" are
where it steepens; whether any are sharp is the same mode-issuance question.)

## Payoff 4 — a physics-native form in the causal set (not just analogy)
In CSG, an element is **final relative to its past immediately** (the covariant J⁺ finality we proved),
but **final relative to the whole structure only asymptotically** (its geometric role settles as
descendants accrue). So finality is naturally stratified by **causal depth / descendant-completeness**,
and μ grades it: reversing an element with few descendants is cheap (metastable); reversing one the
whole structure depends on is expensive (deep-final). Joe's blockchain layers, native to the causet.

## Payoff 5 — the deepest layer is the SOC / self-legitimization layer
L-deep = the collective's ongoing investment = a **self-organized dynamic equilibrium**, not a static
guarantee (hence hard-forkable). This is the R3/R4/R6 self-legitimization layer of the incentive-
selection candidate: "finally final" only while the collective keeps choosing it — a self-organized
critical state, not a set-point. If genuine becoming lives anywhere, it is here (a living consensus,
not a frozen block).

## Honest caveats
- Likely a **continuous μ-gradient**, not truly discrete levels; "levels" are effective regimes.
- **Does NOT escape the block-universe absorber or the D-FORK by itself:** layered *disclosure* is
  still disclosure until the mode-issuance threshold makes the deep layer genuine becoming. The
  stratification reorganizes the finality structure; it does not, alone, earn "becoming."
- **Which layer is the lab "definite outcome"?** Probably L1 (redundancy/objectivity), not L-deep
  (cosmological). Keep the measurement mapping honest about which layer it invokes.
