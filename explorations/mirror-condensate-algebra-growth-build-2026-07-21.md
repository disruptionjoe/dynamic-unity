# Mirror-sector condensate — the constructive build

## The swing (one sentence)

Build the compactifying mirror-sector condensate as an **actual object** — the `ker(Γ)` BCS gap
`Δ`, its BdG spectrum on both branches, and the `Sp(32,32;H) → Sp(32)×Sp(32)` isotropy jump its
adjoint VEV drives — and test whether its **condensation across the roll `N = e^{4p}`** is a
genuine **source-forced record-ALGEBRA growth** (E057 **W1** non-isomorphic algebra growth or
**W2** new source-generated admissibility predicate), the witness that would flip DU's D-FORK from
*disclosure* to *becoming*.

## Answer up front (honest grade)

**PARTIAL.** The condensate builds (it is a real object, §2–§3), and its condensation across the
roll delivers something **strictly more than the null**: a genuine **admissibility-predicate
GENESIS** — the good-stable grading passes from **non-existent** (`F` empty) to **existent** (`F`
nonempty, unique) as `p` crosses the condensation point. That is a W2-*flavored* event, and it is
**not** what a vanilla fixed-action symmetry-breaking gives (§5.4). So the condensate does **not**
read as pure disclosure; it clears the "beats-the-null" bar.

But it **fails both win-bars**, and it fails them for computed structural reasons, not by
stipulation:

1. **Productivity bar — FAILED.** `F: empty→nonempty` is the property "the isotropy image becomes
   relatively compact," and that property is **monotone / absorbing** under further symmetry
   breaking (a subgroup of a compact-image group still has compact image, Prop 1). So the genesis
   fires **at most once** along a monotone-breaking roll: the admissibility count goes `0 → 1` and
   **caps at 1**. Bounded ⇒ a fixed `A_∞` (the condensed-phase admissible algebra) factors every
   `A_n` past `p_c` ⇒ fixed-H ⇒ FTS. There is no productive cascade, and the arena **structurally
   blocks** one.

2. **Source-forced bar — FAILED.** *Which* branch the condensation lands on — good (grading born)
   vs pathological (complex spectrum, no sensible vacuum, no grading) — is selected by **one Krein
   sign** that W211 proved **Gödel-independent of GU**. It is **external / imported**, not
   generated by the B5 source action; and a single bit selecting between two pre-computable
   branches leaves `|Θ_eff| ≤ 2` = finite. So even the one genesis is **externally-contingent**,
   not B5-internal — it fails the "source-generated" qualifier of W2.

**Net:** on the strict rubric (source-forced *and* productive), the **fixed-action absorber wins**
the win-question — consistent with the d-fork §5 headwind that a fixed action generically cannot
grow its observable algebra. What the build *adds* to that verdict is a **precise localization**:
it identifies the *exact* place the operative-C condensate escapes the pure-disclosure reading (the
`F: empty→nonempty` genesis), computes *exactly* why that escape is one-shot (compact-image
absorption) and *exactly* why it is externally-gated (the Gödel-independent selector), and thereby
**sharpens the flip-witness** into two concrete, now-named sub-obstructions a future construction
must beat.

From d-fork §3 (re-verified, not imported): DU is Gödelian for its case **iff no fixed tuple
`A_∞` factors every `A_n` while preserving records** — iff the roll **source-forces** genuinely
new observable/admissibility **types** that are not a restriction, subalgebra, coarse-graining, or
value-selection inside a fixed `A_∞`. E057's decisive caveat governs: *"Merely increasing
dimension, adding an environment, discovering a better effective model, or updating instrumentation
does not count. The growth must be **source-forced**, not a modeler's or observer's access
expansion."*

The object under test is the good-stable **admissibility predicate**: "there exists an invariant
positive majorant / an admissible fundamental symmetry `J` on the vacuum's isotropy
representation." Proposition 1 (W224 / HARDENING-REPORT) makes this a sharp, computable predicate:

> `F_H(η)` is nonempty **exactly** when the image of the isotropy group `H` has relatively
> compact closure.

So the admissibility-type count is `admissibility_count = 1` if `F` is nonempty (and, in the W219
compact-Cartan case, **unique** — `F_K` has dimension 0), and `= 0` if `F` is empty. Record-algebra
growth, made executable: does the roll drive `admissibility_count` **productively** upward (new
predicates begetting new predicates — no fixed `A_∞`), or does it stabilize at a finite ceiling (a
fixed `A_∞` absorbs it)?

## 2. Build the condensate as an actual object (W216), both branches

Anti-toy requirement: the condensate is exhibited, not sketched. Per `ker(Γ)` null pair the BdG
Hamiltonian is a `2×2` Krein block, and the **whole content** is the branch dichotomy (probe §1):

  (`ηH = H†η` verified). Spectrum `±√(ξ² + Δ²)` — **real**, gapped below by `Δ`, both
  quasiparticle Krein types `+1`. A sensible gapped condensate.
- **Pathological branch (C not operative).** `H = ξτ₃ + iΔτ₂`, Krein-self-adjoint w.r.t.
  `η = τ₃`. Spectrum `±√(ξ² − Δ²)` — **complex for every `|ξ| < Δ`** (down to `ξ=0`,

The self-consistent gap is nonzero for any attractive `g > 0` (Cooper): with `R_s=2, g=0.5,
N₀=1`, the band is capped by the essential-spectrum gap `W = μ_c = 9/(2R_s) = 2.25` and

```
Δ* = W / sinh(1/(g N₀)) = 0.620,   E_cond = −½ N₀ Δ*² = −0.192 < 0   (condensed = TRUE vacuum),
Δ* R_s = 1.24 < 9/2   (W175/W216 validity gate: C-operator bounded, spectrum real).
```

All of this is machine-checked (probe §1, 11 checks). The gap **magnitude** is fit-gated (`g, N₀,
R_s` reconstruction-grade, exactly as W216 states); the **existence**, the **real-vs-complex
dichotomy**, and the **validity gate** are structural.

So the condensation is the transition **`F: empty → nonempty`** — the good-stable grading is
**born**, not refined. This is precisely the object W224 named as the missing piece, now built and

## 5. The two win-bars, each with the obstruction computed

### 5.1 Productivity — blocked by compact-image absorption

For a Gödelian win the genesis must be **productive**: a cascade of new admissibility predicates,
each source-forcing the next. Test the obstruction (probe §4). Prop 1 makes `F`-nonempty equivalent
to "isotropy image relatively compact." Further symmetry breaking **shrinks** the isotropy to a
subgroup; a subgroup of a relatively-compact-image group **still** has relatively-compact image.
Therefore **once `F` is nonempty it stays nonempty** — the property is *absorbing*, and you cannot
return to `F`-empty by breaking (that would require *enlarging* the isotropy to something
non-compact, which condensation never does). A simulated post-condensation cascade
`[1,1,1,1,1,1,1]` confirms **zero** further geneses.

> **Structural result:** `F: empty→nonempty` is a **monotone one-way jump**, so
> admissibility-genesis fires **at most once** along a monotone-breaking roll. The count cannot be
> productive. The arena does not merely fail to supply a cascade — it **structurally forbids** one.

### 5.2 Source-forced — the operative-C branch cuts against, not for

The genuinely interesting hope was the operative-C branch: the branch selector is **not**
computable from the fixed action (defeating the naive "phase transition is computable from the
action" absorber). The build confirms that half: the same fixed action is consistent with **both**
branches, so the outcome is **not a function of the action** (probe §5.1). The model-theorist's
verdict — *hosts, does not derive*.

But that same fact is fatal to source-forcing. If the action does not determine the branch, the
determining datum is **external** — and W211 proved that datum (one Krein sign) is
**Gödel-independent of GU**: **imported, not generated by B5**. W2 requires the new predicate to be
**source-generated**; an external oracle bit generates nothing internally. Worse for the
Gödelian reading: a single bit selects between **two pre-computable branches**, so `|Θ_eff| ≤ 2`
= **finite** — FTS *by the count*, even granting the oracle. A completed finite oracle is not the
productive (non-c.e.) source the Gödelian regime demands.

> **The operative-C branch cuts against the win.** It buys "not-action-computable" (real, and it
> is why this is not pure disclosure) at the cost of "externally-imported" — the exact opposite of
> source-forced. Being un-derivable-from-the-action is necessary for a genuine issuance, but it is
> not sufficient; here it is realized as *external contingency*, not *internal generativity*.

### 5.3 What the build DOES clear (why it is PARTIAL, not NO-GROWTH)

Against PC-SSB (ordinary Higgsing, `compact→compact`, `F` nonempty throughout, **zero** geneses),
the condensate has **two** things a vanilla fixed-action SSB cannot claim (probe §5.4): (i) a real
admissibility-**genesis** (`F: empty→nonempty`), a birth of the admissibility predicate rather than
a value-selection inside an already-admissible algebra; and (ii) an outcome that is **not
action-computable**. Both are genuine, and both are why the honest grade is **PARTIAL** rather than
a flat NO-GROWTH: the symmetric phase is not "a different realized state within a valid theory" — it
is a phase with **no valid admissible theory at all**, and the roll brings one into existence. That
is more than disclosure of a pre-existing observable. It is just not *productive*, and not
*source-internal*.
