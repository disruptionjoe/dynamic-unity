## What changed scientifically

### 1. The half-power is class-conditional, not forced by extensivity

The Bianconi action admits at least two natural dissipative completions with
different trajectories but the same tested local stationary point. That is
useful robustness, but it does not select physical time evolution.

More importantly:

```text
iid slope                   -0.500000
short-range mixing slope    -0.499772
long-range r^-0.5 slope     -0.245013
global-mode slope            0.000000
```

The portable result is therefore:

> `Lambda_eff ~ N^-1/2` follows for additive, finite-variance,
> sufficiently mixing fluctuations. It does not follow from an extensive
> trace without a correlation-class assumption.

This lowers the independence of the apparent Bianconi/learning/Sorkin
consilience when those routes reuse the same central-limit mechanism. The
extensive mean also remains unscreened with exact slope `+1`.

### 2. The concept family survives precisely where its invariant speaks

Participation ratio, Shannon/KL effective count, and Gini/Lorenz all recover
the uniform baseline, point-mass endpoint, and majorization monotonicity. On an
exhaustive four-weight rational grid they make zero errors on 144,402
majorization-comparable pairs.

They reverse rankings on 13,266 of 54,994 incomparable pairs (`24.123%`).
That is not a concept failure: majorization itself does not order those pairs.
It is an exact localization of underdetermination. No physical DU object yet
chooses peak sensitivity, information/code-length sensitivity, or Lorenz
transfer sensitivity.

A target-free replicator-plus-mixing flow then supplies a constructive
existence result:

```text
dp_i/dt = s p_i(p_i - sum_j p_j^2) + mu(1/n - p_i)
rho = n mu / s
```

Below the local `rho=1` instability, an arbitrarily small asymmetry grows into
a persistent concentrated fixed point without a `Lambda_target` term. The
amplitude is independent of the seed size and winner identity, survives
dimension/timestep checks, and disappears under the declared
no-reinforcement/strong-mixing controls.

The debit is decisive: `p_i`, the reinforcement/mixing law, and `rho` remain
conditional posits. This proves a mechanism class can do the work; it does not
identify physical dark energy or source its value.

### 3. A finality knee is not identifiable by sharpness alone

For independent record fidelity
`F=|<e_0|e_1>|^2`, exact unitary dynamics gives

```text
V(R,F) = F^(R/2)
```

and exact global erasure restores visibility to one. The conditional trigger

```text
M(R,F) = -R ln F >= M_c
```

entails

```text
R_c(F) = ceil(M_c / -ln F)
V_c = exp(-M_c/2)
```

plus a history-dependent recovery surface if persistence is also posited. A
multi-fidelity scan separates this from a raw-count threshold in every
non-calibration case.

However, an unconstrained smooth sigmoid on the same exposure statistic fits
the entire finite grid to maximum error `0.001375`. Therefore a fitted knee,
even with hysteresis, does not identify finality. The next work would need a
mechanism-specific observable or an independently justified upper bound on the
smooth null's slope.

## Synthesized next suggestion

Do **not** rerun these three tracks generically. The single highest-leverage
next construction is:

> Extract a basis-invariant influence distribution from a live DU dynamical
> object, then test whether that object selects one formalization and sustains
> nonzero concentration without an imported `rho`.

Two predeclared candidates are enough:

1. normalized spectral contributions to entropy dissipation in the Bianconi
   SPD flow; and
2. normalized record leverage/Fisher contribution in the named
   learning/finality loss.

Compare them on the existing majorization/incomparable-profile surface. Success
would bind Tracks 1 and 2: the dynamics would supply `p_i`, potentially choose
the relevant moment/entropy functional, and expose whether concentration is
transient or persistent. Disagreement would be equally valuable and would
block an identity claim.

### Stop conditions

Stop that next construction if:

- the influence distribution is chosen only to reproduce a favored proxy;
- every nonzero fixed point requires fitting `rho` to Lambda;
- the two physical candidates disagree and no independent observable chooses;
- the distribution becomes undefined at the relevant stationary regime; or
- the construction adds no consequence beyond re-expressing the existing
  entropy or Fisher norm.

Only after a physical influence object survives should the program reopen a
growing-cell/issuance extension. Only after a native screening constraint
appears should it reopen Bianconi as a dark-energy amplitude solution. Only
after a finality-specific discriminator appears should it reopen the knee.
