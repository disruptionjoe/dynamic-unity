# Covariant finality collapse — build+kill synthesis

"The one real problem": how is the instantaneous wavefunction update compatible with the
speed-of-light limit? An independent build and adversarial kill both attacked the account *the
update is the fixing of a finality fact typed on the causal order (J⁺), not on simultaneity.* They
converge object-for-object; this is the synthesis.

## 1. The covariance problem DISSOLVES at relocation grade on a finite causal-order toy (no foliation smuggled; AQFT-net derivation unbuilt)
- **Boost-invariance, shown not asserted.** J⁺-finality incidence is invariant to machine precision
  across 41 boosts; a foliation-ordering positive control provably flips sign. The EPR "which
  collapsed first?" is **malformed** — the wings are causally incomparable in J⁺ in *every* frame.
- **No foliation smuggled (the load-bearing kill attack does not land).** The H¹ "global section of
  finalized bindings" is a **covariant sheaf invariant over the causal partial order**
  (Abramsky–Brandenburger obstruction-to-a-global-section, manifestly Lorentz-invariant) — the
  probe shows the H¹ obstruction is identical across every foliation, while the sliced
  "which-finalized-first" readout is frame-dependent, and that sliced object is exactly the naive
  `dR` frontier DU already discards. `foliation_smuggled: false`.
- **Matches (does not derive) the Sorkin/Fewster–Verch admissible restriction — a finite three-region qubit toy, not a net-level derivation.** Three-region toy: a
  foliation/global-Lüders update fabricates the spacelike co-collapse and leaks ~1 bit across
  spacelike separation (Sorkin's impossible measurement); the causal-order finality update forbids
  the link and leaks **exactly 0 bits**.
- **The third position is REAL and distinct:** objective (unlike relational-QM/QBism — the H¹ fact
  is observer-independent) yet not a process (unlike GRW/CSL — no modified Schrödinger term). An
  under-occupied position, not a collapse into a known one.

## 2. The honest ceiling — relocation grade, no becoming (the loud concession)
What is *earned* is **B-theoretic block-structural objectivity**, NOT objective becoming —
corroborated three independent ways: TaF's own audit (reads as B-theory; the physical-arrow layer is
heavily absorbed); TI's CompletionClass (block-level H¹ is `GLOBAL_ONTOLOGICAL_ABSORPTION` via
`completed_history` — an **absorbing** class, not a new physical one); and a model-theoretic
hosts-not-derives check. So as an interpretation it is **block-compatible and predicts identically to
QM** — an *explanatory* upgrade over Shimony's peaceful coexistence (descriptive → explanatory,
at relocation grade), but no new prediction. Concordant with Bet #1. The "malformed-question" move is a
legitimate *resolution* of the ordering question with **zero predictive purchase**.

## 3. The advance — a conditionally covariant-by-construction deviation (PRED-DU-002)
New physics needs finality to be a real sharp threshold (the mode-issuance unlock). *Under that
antecedent:*
- The deviation is a **redundancy-gated recoverability knee** — the *same observable* as PRED-DU-001,
  a collapse-model **genus** but a distinct **species** (NOT continuous spontaneous
  localization/emission).
- It **can be covariant-by-construction**: the trigger is the causal-order **redundancy count**, a
  frame-independent invariant — genuinely **evading the Pearle/Bedingham frame-dependent-noise
  tension** that makes relativistic GRW/CSL hard. This is the prize: a covariant objective-collapse
  candidate.
- **Two blockers, one shared, one its own:** (i) doubly-gated on the unbuilt mode-issuance threshold
  (shared with PRED-DU-001); (ii) the **locality-of-update proviso** — the finalization must be a

## 4. Anti-conflation — two different sheaves, two different H¹ (keep typed apart)
The account rests on distinguishing two sheaves that both carry an "H¹," and they must not be merged:
- **Finality sheaf** — H¹ must **VANISH** for a single shared reality (= no fork = the "unit of
  account that keeps the system from splitting" of the incentive-selection candidate).
- **Contextuality / hidden-value sheaf** (Abramsky–Brandenburger) — H¹ is **NONZERO** for entangled
  states (CHSH = −2√2; Bell). This is what makes it non-classical.
The theory needs the **first to vanish and the second not to**. Hence "one finalized record" is
explicitly **not** a hidden-variable value assignment — it is the joint-record section at the common
*future*, not a value-section over a spacelike slice.
