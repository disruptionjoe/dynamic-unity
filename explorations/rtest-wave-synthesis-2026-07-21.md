## The unified finding — one obstruction, one forward object
Read the last two columns down the table: it is the **same object every time**.

- **The obstruction is uniformly a FIXED SET closed under a FIXED operator** — a fixed part-set
  (R4), a finite type-set (R2/R3), a scale-free law with no scale (R6a), a fixed rule whose strength
  must be tuned (Meta/KR), a fixed count that only additively grows (R6b). This is the wave-2
  fixed-H/Bogoliubov absorber, re-instantiated five independent ways.
- **The crossing is uniformly a SOURCE-INTERNAL MINT** — grow the part-set / alphabet / modes /
  rules / scale **from within**: R4's "source-internal part-mint," R2/R3's source-selected type-III,
  R6a's self-sourced scale, wave-2's "grow ker(Γ)," and W2's "author a new admissibility predicate."
  All one object: a source that issues new *particulars*, not reshuffles fixed ones.

**R4's decisive sharpening: the blocker is NOT teleology.** The teleology audit is clean and its
positive control fires — a *goal-injected* rule also fails to break the finite ceiling; it just fills
the fixed type-space deeper. Only an *external part-mint* broke it (and that fails R4 by import). So
the missing ingredient is **not** agency/intentionality (the frightening part) — it is a
**non-teleological source-internal mint**. That is genuinely clarifying: de-teleologization is *not*
the wall; the fixed alphabet is.

## Why it is one bottleneck (the anatomy)
Every mechanism delivers **general/structural** content for free (a law is, by nature, general) and
stalls at the **particular** (a scale, a selection, a value, a new type). A general thing cannot
single out a particular except by (a) **importing** it (tuning / external bit / fixed initial
condition — what every stalled swing had to do) or (b) **self-generating** it (a source that authors
particulars from itself). This is the dynamic twin of the static type wall, and the ancient
law-vs-initial-condition split. The bottleneck is the one object that would do (b): the unbuilt,
self-generating **B5 source action**. Its three roles: it **concentrates all the hardness in one
place** (everything else is native); it is the **honesty firewall** (it is where the discipline
refuses to let an import wear the costume of a derivation); and it **is the program's thesis
localized** — "can a source self-generate its particulars?" = "is becoming real?".

## Why it is not hopeless — the escape precedent
The wall is **class-relative** (true within scale-free / fixed-set laws), not proven global. There is
a real physics precedent for the escape: **dimensional transmutation** — QCD generates ΛQCD, a genuine
mass *scale*, from a *scale-free, dimensionless* theory purely by RG running under the trace anomaly.
No scale imported; a scale self-generated. This makes R6a's parting suggestion concrete: build a
source whose scale self-generates transmutation-style, tied to the **D-spectral gap**. The honest
alternative verdict remains open: the particulars may be irreducibly imported (laws + boundary data,
like all physics), and the D-FORK's non-computability-in-general is exactly why this can be settled
only by *building* the source and checking for a becoming-witness (R5), not by cleverness.

## Bearing on the Lane-1 anchor (Λ~1/√N)
The anchor stands: the ½ exponent is native/consilient and dissolves the fine-tuning. The wave
sharpens the *open* half: R6b confirms the 120 orders are gone (generic O(1)); R6a shows a
set-point-free route **sources the exponent** but is **scale-free**, and — the finding with teeth —
**non-additivity (what R6 needs) and a self-averaging 1/√N are in tension** (non-additive SOC gives a
heavy-tailed 3/2; 1/√N returns only under additive counting = the wave-1 import). So the coefficient
needs a *second, self-sourced scale-fixing ingredient* (the D-spectral-gap / transmutation route).
The anchor's `honest_standing` in LANES is updated to record this.
