## The elephant, named (the integrated plain-English story)
**Reality is a self-authoring ledger.** There is one thing — a growing record of what has happened,
woven into a causal web (a causal set) — and everything we call fundamental is a different *view* of
that one thing:
- **Space and time** are the order and the accumulation of the records.
- **Dark energy** is the statistical error-bar of a universe with a *finite* number of records — tiny
  precisely because there are so many (Λ ~ 1/√N). The worst fine-tuning in physics becomes arithmetic.
- **Quantum measurement** is a record *becoming final* — settled relative to its own causal past —
  which is why it needs no faster-than-light collapse and no universal "now."
- **Physical law** is the ledger's *protocol*: the rules for which events are admissible.

And it runs like a **viable system**: observers record (S1), share and reconcile (S2/S3) into one
agreed present (shared reality = the ledger hasn't forked), and — *if it is truly alive* — **authors
new possibility (System 4)**, ratified into new law and rate (S5).

We can see all of that. The one part we cannot yet see — **the trunk** — is the organ of authorship:
the source that *mints genuinely new content* instead of rearranging what exists. That organ is
exactly what separates a **living, becoming** universe from a **frozen block that merely looks alive.**

## May we call it an elephant? — YES, with one precise honesty
Everything native and consilient we have fixes the *shape* beyond reasonable doubt: it is
elephant-*shaped*. But the missing trunk is not cosmetic — it is the single feature that distinguishes
a live elephant (genuine becoming / a functioning System 4) from a perfect statue of one (disclosure /
a frozen block). Every result we've banked is consistent with *both*. So the honest blockbuster
headline is **not** "reality is a self-authoring ledger" (over-claim) but:

> **Reality is shaped exactly like a self-authoring informational cosmos whose dark energy is the
> fingerprint of its own size — and whether it is genuinely authoring (alive) or merely frozen (a
> statue) is the single open, decidable question, identical to "does it have a System 4."**

That is *stronger* than most unification programs can honestly claim, because it names its own
falsifier and its one open bit — and that bit is **buildable and checkable**, not metaphysical.
