# SWING-DU-SCI-01 — three-track council swing

## Decision

Run three bounded investigations under one comparison contract:

1. **Entropy-geometry completion robustness:** determine which parts of the Bianconi
   `sigma Tr ln G` result survive legitimate changes of dynamics, geometry, and
   fluctuation correlation.
2. **Influence-redistribution abduction:** test a family of faithful
   `CONCEPT-DU-001` formalizations rather than treating one articulation as the
   concept.
3. **Conditional finality phenomenology:** assume a labeled sharp
   redundancy/finality threshold and determine whether it yields quantitative
   content beyond inserting a knee by hand.

The swing does not require a microscopic source before these constructions may be
explored. It applies the durable
`lab/process/conditional-and-abductive-research-contract.md`: every posit,
completion choice, and discriminator is visible. Honesty governs the grade and
banking boundary, not admission to exploration.

## The shared candidate contract

Every track emits a JSON artifact through
`tests/conditional_candidate_harness.py`. The contract requires:

- the exact question and one or more **typed warrants**;
- labeled assumptions, distinguishing standard structure, project-native
  structure, conditional posits, and imports;
- free choices and a sensitivity test for each;
- equations, observables, comparators, null models, falsifiers, and stop
  conditions;
- a result with explicit remaining uncertainty and executable checks;
- for a concept-family test, the concept invariant, tested formalization, and
  whether failure is local to the formalization or reaches the invariant.

Contract completeness means **legible and comparable**, not correct. The
instrument never promotes a scientific claim and does not create a new standing
verification gate.

## Non-ordinal warrant types

These types answer different questions and must not be collapsed into a single
"amount derived" score:

| Warrant | Relation established |
|---|---|
| `DERIVED` | The conclusion follows from the stated premises by formal or mathematical argument. |
| `CONDITIONALLY_ENTAILED` | A labeled posit plus a specified completion implies a discriminating consequence. |
| `CONSTRUCTIVELY_REALIZED` | An explicit object or dynamics has been built and shown to possess the stated behavior. |
| `ABDUCTIVELY_PREFERRED` | Among declared rivals, one explanation wins a comparative test on compression, independence, robustness, or novel consequences. |
| `STRUCTURAL_ANALOGY` | A typed structural correspondence is present, without an identity claim. |

A candidate may carry multiple warrants. None licenses silently dropping its
premises.

## Heterodox concept-family rule

A formalization belongs to a concept family only if it carries the predeclared
concept invariant. Failure then closes that **formalization**, not the concept.
Concept-level closure requires either:

1. a direct no-go on the invariant; or
2. exhaustion of a materially diverse family whose failures share a mode traced
   to the invariant.

Conversely, swapping proxies after every failure without preserving the invariant
is not heterodox preservation; it is story-shopping.

## Comparative-abduction surface

The synthesis compares candidates on:

1. **Explanatory compression:** independent observations accounted for per free
   choice.
2. **Evidence independence:** whether apparent consilience reuses the same
   assumption, dataset, or central-limit mechanism.
3. **Discriminating novelty:** a result a named null does not already produce.
4. **Robustness:** behavior that survives legitimate completion or
   formalization changes.
5. **Progressivity:** whether the swing reduces arbitrary choices or adds risky
   consequences.

These are reported as evidence, not compressed into a false precision score.

## Cross-track admission boundary

An explicit model with labeled assumptions, equations, and a real discriminator
is admitted to exploration. A result reaches `BANK_REVIEW_ONLY` only after the
track-specific robustness tests, standard-rival comparison, and independent
claim audit; this swing cannot bank it by itself.

## Swing-level stop rule

Stop rather than circle when a track:

- produces only behavior shared by its null;
- needs a new unconstrained parameter after each failed test;
- loses its signature under modest, physically legitimate perturbations; or
- cannot name an observation that distinguishes the construction from its
  antecedent inserted by hand.

Negative results remain useful: they localize the failure to a completion,
formalization, or conditional posit instead of being inflated into a global
concept kill.
