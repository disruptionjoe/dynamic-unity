## Class 1 — Standing adversaries (wire them so they BITE)

**Council reading (S4 lead, S2/S1 concur).** These six are the highest-value thing in the
harvest, because they are *exactly* banking-gate kills — the charter says honesty bites there,
and here are six in-house skeptics the two crown-jewel banks (the transducer, the DE-amplitude)

to count. **They install first.** But per the charter's sovereignty rule they are *DU's own*
pre-registered kills, re-verified here — not adopted because a sibling asserted them.

**The mechanism that makes them bite** — three coupled pieces, not a doc:

1. **A standing-absorbers register** — one canonical file, `lab/process/standing-absorbers-register.md`
   (method/governance surface, alongside the parsimony ledger). Each absorber gets: what it

   This is pre-registration, so a later "the instrument couldn't discriminate" cannot become a
   survival hatch.

   parsimony ledger plus its rigged control." Add one line to its purpose: *maintains the

   job, which is what makes it bite.
3. **A Lane-3 gate** — no transducer or DE-amplitude result advances to a Joe-review draft until
   the register is run and each absorber is marked passed / failed / open against its
   pre-registered criterion.

## Class 5 — Sequencing & risk

**Incorporate FIRST (cheap, protective, charter-aligned — all bite only at the banking gate):**
the standing-absorbers register + Lane-A enforcement duty + type-wall table (Class 1); the three
cheap gates — B5 9-item rubric, bridge units-guard, Krein sign guardrail (3a); the safe-now honesty
methods — verification tiers, `[T]/[E]/[F]`, provenance/deletion test, canon-precedence (2a); the
persona-register pointer + Adversary-C + anti-crank-at-banking (Class 4).

**Incorporate LATER:** the deep work-cards — Bianconi dynamics, B5 return arrow, O-b discharge
(3b); the structural roadmap and H¹/μ framing (3b, as maps/Discovery); and the ai-epistemology
checks that *promote* the 2b calibrators from soft to hard gates — run as DU matures, before those
methods become load-bearing.

**The single biggest over-import risk (S6, endorsed by the Chairman):** a moonshot that inherits
five repos' full apparatus **ossifies before it explores.** The charter's *first* non-negotiable is
open exploration — honesty bites at **banking**, not at exploration. The specific failure mode:
making the kill/verification machinery (absorbers, pre-registration, pair-and-null, the whole
verification ladder, the persona clusters) **mandatory at the exploration gate** instead of only at
the banking gate — which converts "honesty, not conservatism" into precisely the conservatism the
charter rejects, and strangles the divergent Discovery / coherence-first sweeps (W155-style) the
program depends on. **Mitigation, baked into every item above:** gates fire at banking only;
Discovery stays free; the 2b methods run soft and are promoted only by ai-epistemology; the workflow
library is imported *lazily* (only when a lane needs it), never wholesale.

**The honest note (S4):** the standing adversaries mean the transducer **starts behind.** After
incorporation DU is better-armed and better-disciplined, but its flagship claim now faces a
*pre-registered six-absorber gauntlet* plus a sign-selection no-go (no conserving dynamics derives
the DE sign) it has **not yet passed.** Incorporation advances the physics zero inches — it raises
and honestly marks the hill the transducer must climb. For a truth-seeking, verdict-agnostic program
that is not a cost; it is the point.

## Chairman synthesis

### The 3–5 highest-value incorporation moves

1. **Stand up the standing-absorbers register as a Lane-3 banking gate with a Lane-A owner.** The
   single highest-value move: it operationalizes "honesty bites at the banking gate" and directly
   guards the two crown-jewel banks (transducer, DE-amplitude) with six pre-registered kills. Pair
   it with the **type-wall table** — the cheapest artifact that most reliably catches a silent
   inflation (`N`-count vs `N`-rate, record-accretion vs finality, `d=0` trap).
2. **Install the three cheap gates now:** P2C's 9-item B5 spec as Channel 1.3's acceptance rubric;
   TaF's bridge units-guard as Lane-2.1's admission gate; the Krein sign guardrail on Channel 1.1
   (don't chase deriving the DE sign from conserving dynamics). Near-zero cost, high protection,
   removes a pre-mapped dead end.
3. **Adopt the honesty-infrastructure methods now; make the kill-strength calibrators DU's *first*
   ai-epistemology subjects.** Verification tiers, `[T]/[E]/[F]`, the provenance/deletion test
   (which *is* sovereignty made concrete), and canon-precedence go in immediately. Two-outcomes-
   both-lethal, pair-and-null/rigged-ledger, and the CompletionClass null-class run **soft**, hard-
   gating only after ai-epistemology rules them safeguards rather than conservative leans — which is
   DU's Passion applied to its own apparatus.
4. **Create the persona register as a pointer, not a fork** (honoring ISSUE-0030): point into the
   RCCM central registry + the TaF panel; declare a DU dynamic-physics board; seat Adversary-C
   (attack the strongest form) and a mandatory anti-crank council at banking; propose the three
   genuinely-new seats (tachyonic dynamics, mirror/hidden-sector, B5 source-action) upstream, use
   them locally now.
5. **Open the two lead work-cards from prime material:** (a) supply the **Bianconi σ-habitat** its
   dynamics/Hamiltonian and test `σ·Tr ln G` as the **volume→Λ link** — the program's raison
   d'être, re-verified for fit; (b) the **B5 return arrow**, the named unbuilt piece.

### The single biggest over-import risk to avoid

**Hard-gating the exploration frontier.** Inheriting five repos' verification machinery and firing
it at the *exploration* gate — rather than only at the *banking* gate — would ossify the moonshot
before it explores and turn "honesty, not conservatism" into conservatism. Keep every gate at
banking; keep Discovery free; adopt kill-methods as hypotheses under ai-epistemology, never as
imported orthodoxy; import the workflow library lazily.

### Where DU stands after incorporation (honest one-liner)

**Better-armed and better-disciplined, but with its flagship claim now facing a pre-registered
six-absorber gauntlet and a sign-selection no-go it has not yet passed — incorporation advances the
physics zero inches; it raises and honestly marks the hill the transducer must climb, which is
exactly what a truth-seeking program should want.**
