The portfolio pivot is right, but the unit of work is wrong.

The coupled `HC-DU-011 + HC-DU-022` direction deserves to be the next major
**program** because it is the only live route that can attack three expensive
failures at once:

1. metric-seeded geometry rather than order-first geometry;
2. scale-free causal memory that tracks instead of accelerating; and
3. a passive readout with no return arrow into growth.

It is not yet one build-ready ticket. The detailed predecessor already prices
the direction as nine obligations: growth/action, order-first count, signed
source, return arrow, generated scale, geometry recovery, response/noise
completion, forward observable, and rival sweeps. Calling that whole object
“the next major build” hides the first decision and invites a large integration
whose failure would not localize.

The smallest next product should therefore be:

> **A frozen order-first causal-scale admission passport plus an executable
> small-causet kernel harness.**

That product must decide whether any candidate has (a) a genuinely
label-invariant transition law, (b) an autonomous feedback variable rather than
an `N`-scheduled knob, and (c) a regulator-stable dimensionless hierarchy tied
to one declared microscopic unit. Geometry, CTP response/noise, and cosmology
come only after those three gates.

My reconciliation is:

> **`HC-DU-011 + HC-DU-022` priority upheld; “single next build” narrowed to a
> staged research program; first build specified below.**

No physics claim, prediction, bank, or seed follows from this memo.

## 3. Bounded contested-finding swing

### 3.1 Dimensional-transmutation unit audit

This is an analytic derivation, not a numerical proxy.

Let a growth law use only:

- a finite order `C_n`;
- dimensionless cardinalities and order invariants `x_n`;
- a dimensionless coupling `g_n`; and
- dimensionless transition probabilities
  `P(C_n -> C_(n+1) | x_n,g_n)`.

An autonomous running law can generate a dimensionless crossover:

```text
dg/d ln n = beta(g)

ln(N_star/N_ref)
  = integral_[g_ref]^[g_star] dg / beta(g).
```

That can be a genuine generated hierarchy if `N_star/N_ref` is stable under
changes of regulator and does not come from an inserted threshold.

It cannot by itself generate physical units. A duration, length, or mass still
has the form

```text
tau_star = ell_0 * F(g_ref, order invariants)

or

m_star = mu_0 * G(g_ref, order invariants),
```

where `ell_0` or `mu_0` is a declared unit-bearing reference. Under a common
change of units `ell_0 -> c ell_0`, every physical duration changes by `c`.
Dimensionless order data cannot prevent that transformation.

This does not deny dimensional transmutation. It states its correct commercial
acceptance criterion:

> Generate a large, regulator-stable **dimensionless hierarchy** relative to
> one honestly declared microscopic unit; do not promise an absolute unit from
> dimensionless order alone.

The existing spectral-flow kill is the exact negative control. Its causal-set
spectral scale moves as `rho^(1/d)` with the discretization. The positive
control lets the coupling run with the regulator and keeps the transmuted
combination invariant. Therefore the present wording “endogenously generates a
dimensional memory scale” should be operationalized as:

> **generates an RG-invariant memory hierarchy relative to a declared
> discreteness unit, before any `H0` or cosmological calibration.**

## 4. Ranked next-actions roadmap

### Action 1 — freeze `HC-DU-011A`, the order-growth admission passport

**Concrete output**

A candidate contract defining:

- finite unlabeled causal-set state;
- allowed precursor/transition class;
- transition probabilities or action;
- relabeling/discrete-covariance action;
- past-cardinality and every other state variable;
- source/resource ledger;
- fixed-history, target-scheduled, and metric-seeded rivals; and
- the exact failure scope of every gate.

Pair it with an exhaustive or sampled small-causet harness that checks
normalization, transitive closure, past immutability, and equality of path
weights for isomorphic unlabeled outcomes.

**Falsifier / stop**

Stop the candidate if its transition weight depends on birth labels, supplied
coordinates, a desired dimension, an `N`-scheduled tuning table, or future
history.

**Why now**

Every later result is uninterpretable without knowing whether the order law is
a law or a target-conditioned generator.

### Action 2 — run a two-family feedback/transmutation assay

Admit at most two materially different mechanism families initially:

1. an autonomous running coupling driven by order invariants; and
2. a spectral/transfer gap that feeds back into transition weights.

Do not admit a third family until one of the first two earns a nontrivial
survivor or both fail for different reasons.

**Concrete output**

For each family:

- explicit feedback/beta equation;
- declared microscopic unit;
- multi-regulator sequence;
- generated dimensionless crossover/hierarchy;
- fixed-coupling cutoff-tracking control;
- feedback-off ablation; and
- resource/noise accounting.

**Falsifier / stop**

Stop if the scale tracks the cutoff, if the coupling must be supplied as a
function of `N`, if the desired crossover is an explicit threshold, or if the
feedback has no observable difference from a fixed stochastic law under a
matched resource budget.

**Why now**

This is the cheapest direct test of `HC-DU-022` and blocks another large
scale-free cosmology run.

### Action 3 — demand a geometry basin, not another crossing

**Concrete output**

Run surviving growth laws across size, regulator, seed, and initial-condition
families. Measure at least:

- Myrheim–Meyer ordering-fraction dimension;
- longest-chain scaling;
- rank-layer width;
- interval abundance or another held-out order observable; and
- the generated hierarchy from Action 2.

The target is a stable basin or scaling trajectory, not a strength value that
passes through `d=4` or another desired point.

**Falsifier / stop**

Stop if geometry requires the feedback strength to run by hand with `N`, if
only one target-used estimator improves, or if the result disappears under a
legitimate regulator/initial-state change.

**Why now**

The existing CSG receipt already shows that order-only selection can move
geometry. The next information gain is stability, not another lever sweep.

### Action 4 — build the return arrow and use influence objects as telemetry

**Concrete output**

Let an independently defined raw response from the grown order feed the next
transition law. Compare against:

- feedback off;
- replayed fixed history;
- shuffled response;
- exact-duplication records; and
- matched resource/noise budgets.

Carry the Bianconi and record influence profiles only as secondary diagnostics:
completion sensitivity, modal localization, record surprise, and possible
phase-transition warning. Do not map them to `Lambda`.

**Falsifier / stop**

Stop if feedback changes only a normalized profile while the raw response,
geometry, and hierarchy do not change; if exact duplication looks like
independent evidence; or if the same behavior is reproduced by a fixed-law
replay.

**Why now**

This is the first point at which the influence work can change a decision
without reopening proxy selection.

### Action 5 — only after Actions 1–4, pay for response/noise and cosmology

**Concrete output**

A causal response/noise action or equivalent completion, stress consistency,
finite-`k` stability, common-past spectrum, and then a background comparison.
Likelihood work comes only after the background survives the existing
early-universe and tracker controls.

**Falsifier / stop**

Stop before likelihood if the generated component is always on, tracks the
dominant background, lacks positive noise covariance, requires a selected
stochastic seed, or imports `m≈H0`.

**Why now**

It is not now. Its place in the sequence prevents expensive downstream work
from masquerading as progress on an unbuilt source.

## 7. Missed or underused outcomes

### 7.1 The influence wave produced quality-control instruments

The best near-term use of the influence objects is not another dark-energy
identity. It is instrumenting the next growth build:

- Bianconi completion disagreement is a mobility-selection alarm;
- vanishing raw dissipation beside concentrated normalized shape is a
  shape/scale accounting test;
- record score energy is an event-surprise and stress-attribution diagnostic;
- exact duplication is an anti-fake-information-growth control; and
- the old incomparable pair is a ready-made held-out selector case once a
  physical response exists.

The current synthesis correctly stops the branch, but it understates this
instrumentation value.

### 7.2 The record object exposed an evidence-accounting problem

The normalized record profile cannot distinguish independent accretion from
exact copies. Any order-first model that calls every birth “new information”
needs a raw resource or conditional-independence ledger. This is commercially
important because otherwise simulated growth can look successful by bookkeeping
alone.

### 7.3 The CSG result already pays for one uncertainty

We do not need another demonstration that order-only selection can move a
geometry estimator. That lever exists at finite `N`. The live uncertainty is
whether an autonomous law creates a basin and whether the same mechanism
generates a regulator-stable hierarchy.

### 7.4 Portable reproducibility is not identical to local byte stability

The new wave artifacts are byte-stable on the pinned local environment. Two
older numerical artifacts reproduced their scientific verdicts but not their
last-bit JSON bytes on this host. A portable receipt should distinguish:

- exact symbolic/integer fields;
- canonicalized floating outputs;
- tolerance-based semantic checks; and
- platform-specific raw numerics.

That is a concrete future use for `HC-DU-009`, not a reason to rerun the
science now.
