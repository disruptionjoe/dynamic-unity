## 2. The proof attempt ‚Äî three of the four links ARE theorems

### 2.1 immutable ‚áí inflationary ‚áí **has a fixpoint** (the quine). THEOREM.

Model the accretion as `Œ¶(R) = R ‚à™ g(R)` where `g(R)` is the records generated from the current state.
Immutability = **union** = never delete, so `R ‚äÜ Œ¶(R)`: `Œ¶` is **inflationary**. By **Bourbaki-Witt**

**closed under its own generation law** = the self-consistent record set = the **disclosure limit / a
quine**. If `g` is additionally Scott-continuous, the sharper **Kleene fixed-point theorem** gives `R*`
as the sup of the œâ-chain (the accretion *is* the ascending Kleene chain). *Probe Part 1:* the modeled
`Œ¶` (with a deliberately **non-monotone** `g`) is inflationary at every step, reaches `Œ¶(R*)=R*` closed
under `g`; a deleting (non-immutable) control is non-inflationary and **cycles** ‚Äî no closure. **The
"has a fixpoint" link is a theorem**, and cleaner than the claim states (Bourbaki-Witt needs only
*inflationary*, which immutability gives for free).

### 2.2 The Lawvere link, *if L1 is a total point-surjection*. THEOREM ‚Äî but the hypothesis is only fixture-grade.

Lawvere: a point-surjection `e:A‚ÜíB^A` forces **every** `g:B‚ÜíB` to have a fixpoint. *Contrapositive:* a

explicit that L1-as-total-diagonal "lands at fixture grade," blocked at the product-uniform
norm-resolvent obstruction. So the pure-Lawvere route to "L2 external" is **conditional on an unproven
totality**. This matters, because it is exactly the crack the refutation walks through.

### 2.3 The order-theoretic reading of immutability ‚Äî orientability. (Sets up both the refute and the rescue.)

Immutability has a sharper reading than "monotone." *Records don't un-happen* means the accretion
carries a **globally-consistent direction** (a well-defined, non-vanishing, self-returning "later-than"
field). A globally-consistent direction is an **orientation in the trivial class** (`w‚ÇÅ = 0`): the
accretion is **orientable**, and immutability *is* the choice of orientation. Hold this ‚Äî it is the
hinge.

**The crux the task names:** must the involution act on the **records** (‚áí deletion ‚áí violates
