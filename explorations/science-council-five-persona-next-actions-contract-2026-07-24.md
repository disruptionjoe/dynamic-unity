# Science Council five-persona next-actions contract

## Purpose

Run five independent Science Council work products after `SWING-DU-PHY-02`.
Each persona must say what it would do next, expose the hypotheses and
conjectures motivating that sequence, identify what should stop, and make one
bounded evidence-producing swing at an interpretation it considers weak,
misstated, or prematurely closed.

The five lenses are:

1. orthodox professor;
2. heterodox professor;
3. commercial scientist;
4. wild-frontier scientist; and
5. philosopher of science.

This is parallel work, not a vote. Agreement counts neither as proof nor as a
score. Root integration will preserve material disagreements.

## Required order of work

Each persona works in this order:

1. **Re-audit the receipts.** Inspect the wave contract, pre-result audit,
   synthesis, Track A and Track B interpretations, executable probes, artifacts,
   concept register, live `LANES.yaml`, and the `HC-DU-011 + HC-DU-022`
   hardening-map entries. Do not reason from the summary alone.
2. **Select one contested finding.** State the exact current proposition,
   explain why it may be too strong, too weak, or incorrectly scoped, and name
   what evidence could change it.
3. **Take one bounded swing.** Prefer an analytic derivation, independent
   recomputation, counterexample, toy construction, discriminating comparison,
   or executable probe. If the question cannot yet be evaluated, specify the
   minimum missing object rather than manufacturing a result.
4. **Reconcile the result.** Mark the contested finding as upheld, narrowed,
   overturned, or still not evaluable. Separate concept-level implications from
   formalization-local ones.
5. **Write the next-actions roadmap.** Order actions by dependency and
   information gain, not by persona taste. Every action must have a concrete
   output, falsifier or stop condition, and a reason it belongs now.
6. **Write a hypothesis ledger.** Include candidate hypotheses, conjectures,
   possible claims, current warrant type and grade, decisive test, and what may
   not yet be said.
7. **Write the stop ledger.** Name work that is no longer worth time and the
   exact condition that could reopen it.
8. **Name missed outcomes.** Identify any result whose best scientific use is
   different from the use assigned in the current synthesis.
9. **Take a final unconstrained divergent pass.** While the full context is
   still active, record every additional path the persona genuinely notices and
   considers worth preserving. This is not an invitation to maximize list
   length, manufacture novelty, rank the ideas as current priorities, or make
   them satisfy the roadmap's admission bar. It is a lossless future-memory
   surface for paths that may matter later.

For each divergent item, capture only enough to make it recoverable:

- the idea or question;
- why it might matter;
- the first object, observation, or calculation that would make it less vague;
- any obvious collision with a known stop or prior failed class; and
- status `UNRANKED / UNCOMMITTED / NOT_A_CLAIM`.

## Shared scientific constraints

- `OBJECT-FOUND` is not a selector, scale, cosmological identity, or banked
  claim.
- `NO-NONZERO-PHYSICAL-SCALE-IDENTIFIED` means the wave did not identify one;
  it is not a theorem that none can exist.
- Normalized shape and a unit-bearing raw carrier stay on separate ledgers.
- The record object decomposes observed residual energy, not expected Fisher
  information.
- Euclidean and affine Bianconi completions have only the declared `O(3)`
  action symmetry; the natural-gradient record path has the declared joint
  linear covariance.
- The class-conditional half-power may not be flattened into an unconditional
  derivation.
- No persona may bank, seed, publish, edit shared lane/claim state, or convert
  check counts or council agreement into scientific evidence.

## Persona-specific challenge

### Orthodox professor

Stress-test the asymptotic and statistical interpretation. Ask whether the
replication signature is merely a normalization theorem, whether persistent
Gini or the terminal modal residue has a legitimate diagnostic use, and
whether `CONCEPT-SUPPORTED-BY-LIVE-OBJECTS` is the right evidentiary grade.

Owned output:
`explorations/science-council-orthodox-next-actions-2026-07-24.md`.

### Heterodox professor

Stress-test the equilibrium/closed-system scope. Ask whether vanishing
dissipation is an artifact of relaxation without growth or drive, and whether
a minimal open, driven, or accreting construction changes the branch stop
without inserting a target concentration or cosmological clock.

Owned output:
`explorations/science-council-heterodox-next-actions-2026-07-24.md`.

### Commercial scientist

Stress-test executability and decision value. Turn the current pivot into the
smallest sequence of buildable decision products, and challenge whether
`HC-DU-011 + HC-DU-022` is sufficiently specified to deserve “next major
build.” Identify reusable diagnostics and work that consumes effort without
changing a decision.

Owned output:
`explorations/science-council-commercial-next-actions-2026-07-24.md`.

### Wild-frontier scientist

Stress-test the absence of a constructive bridge. Seek the boldest coherent
mechanism by which order-first growth could select a mobility, generate a
dimensional memory scale, and turn the existing influence objects into
downstream observables. It must expose inserted choices and a cheap kill.

Owned output:
`explorations/science-council-wild-frontier-next-actions-2026-07-24.md`.

### Philosopher of science

Stress-test the inferential state. Ask whether the three-part influence
reopener is one indivisible gate or a staged research program, whether
`NOT_EVALUABLE` and “branch stop” are being used correctly, and whether the
current concept grade distinguishes existence, relevance, and identification.

Owned output:
`explorations/science-council-philosopher-next-actions-2026-07-24.md`.

## File and coordination boundary

Each persona owns only its named memo plus uniquely prefixed optional probe and
artifact files. Personas may read every repository file and run existing
probes, but must not edit `LANES.yaml`, the concept or prediction registers,
shared process documents, another persona's files, or durable memory. Root
performs all synthesis, state changes, staging, and publication.

Every memo must report:

- files created;
- commands or derivations used;
- result of the contested-finding swing;
- roadmap;
- hypothesis and stop ledgers; and
- an unconstrained but non-padded divergent wish list;
- unresolved disagreement with the current synthesis.
