# Dynamic Unity concept register

## The discipline (why a formalization's kill is NOT a concept's kill)
1. **Capture-before-test.** State the concept in its most **articulation-invariant** form, and write it
   down, *before* testing any formalization — so the idea survives a formalization's death.
2. **Name the concept INVARIANT.** The property every *faithful* formalization must share — the
   concept's signature. A candidate formalization is *in the family* iff it carries the invariant.
   Testing a formalization then tests the **articulation**, not the concept.
3. **Enumerate a FAMILY of formalizations. Test the SET,** not the first one that comes to hand.
4. **Local vs global grading.**
   - A failed formalization → **FORMALIZATION-FALSIFIED** (local).
   - The concept stays **CONCEPT-OPEN** until EITHER (a) the family is *exhausted* AND the failures
     share a mode that **traces to the invariant** (→ **CONCEPT-FALSIFIED** — a real, global finding
     worth having), OR (b) a **direct global no-go on the invariant** itself.
   - A surviving formalization → **CONCEPT-SUPPORTED** (by that route).
5. **Only falsification of the INVARIANT closes the concept.** A dead articulation is a dead
   articulation.

## CONCEPT-DU-001 — Λ as higher-order influence-redistribution pressure
- **Concept (articulation-invariant).** Dark energy Λ is neither a fundamental constant nor a
  first-order exponent shift. It is a **higher-order (2nd/3rd-order) structural effect**: the *pressure*
  by which the system's own dynamics (correlations / information / a **mechanism**) reshape the
  effective **influence/weight distribution** across its degrees of freedom, away from the base
  ("natural-law", uniform / one-record-one-vote) allocation. The reshaping is **dynamically/
  mechanistically sourced** (an initial condition), and Λ reads out the deviation.
- **Concept INVARIANT (the signature every faithful formalization must have):**
  (i) Λ = f(deviation of the effective influence-distribution from uniform);
  (ii) f(uniform / no deviation) = the baseline (the Sorkin 1/√N at N_eff = N);
  (iii) **monotone** — Λ grows as influence concentrates;
  (iv) the concentration is **sourced by the system's own higher-order dynamics / a mechanism**, NOT a
  tuned constant.
- **Formalization FAMILY (test the SET):**
  1. **N_eff / participation ratio:** Λ ~ 1/√N_eff, N_eff = (Σw)²/Σw².
  2. **Crossover scale** between additive-(½) and heavy-tail-(3/2) regimes — *the running build+kill;
     the first-order shadow.*
  3. **KL-divergence-from-uniform:** Λ = D(actual ‖ uniform) of the influence-distribution — the clean
     information-theoretic "distance from the natural law."
  4. **Influence function / leverage** (statistics): Λ = a robustness/leverage measure of the curve.
  5. **Shapley / cooperative-game:** Λ = deviation of the Shapley influence-allocation from equal-split.
  6. **Spectral reweighting:** Λ = deviation of the mode-spectrum's effective weighting from flat (the
     DeWitt / spectral-dimension version).
  7. **RG relevant coupling:** Λ = the relevant deviation from the critical fixed point.
  8. **Free-energy / loss effective-sample reweighting** (the Vanchurin learning-dynamics bridge).
- **Grading (2026-07-22, build+kill returned).** Formalization **#2 (crossover) = PARTIAL — LAW derived
  (Λ~1/√N_c, N_c~δ⁻², non-circular; it DISSOLVES the R6a 3/2-vs-½ apparent contradiction into one
  relevant-coupling flow), VALUE imported (δ an initial condition)** — a LOCAL result, **NOT falsified**

  The **Vanchurin-loss route is LITERAL-AT-FLOOR** (non-vacuous multi-D gradient flow on a named loss;

## Divergent-persona formalization family (2026-07-22) — measurable cross-domain reframes
Ten wildly-divergent lenses each reframed CONCEPT-DU-001; the most MEASURABLE distinct additions to the
family (each a way to make "deviation of the effective influence-distribution from uniform" precise):
- **Political-economy (Gini / Lorenz):** Λ = the Lorenz-area / Gini — literally the area between the
  actual-influence curve and the equal (one-vote) line. *Measurable: Gini.* [Joe's exact image; cleanest.]
- **Cosmological backreaction (Buchert):** Λ = the backreaction term `Q_D` from averaging an
  inhomogeneous influence-distribution — the deviation of the averaged dynamics from uniform-FRW.
  *Measurable: Q_D.* [A REAL existing effective-DE mechanism; closest to physics.]
- **Population genetics:** Λ = 1/√N_e, N_e = effective population size (< N by influence-variance,
  Wright). *Measurable: N_e.* [The rigorous N_eff.]
- **ML attention:** Λ = 1/T (softmax temperature / sharpening); uniform at T→∞, concentrated at T→0.
  *Measurable: attention entropy.* [The mechanism-knob; the Vanchurin/loss bridge.]
- **Information theory:** Λ = KL(actual ‖ uniform) — distance from the natural law in bits. *Measurable: KL.*
- **Ecology (Hill numbers):** Λ = 1/√(effective species #) or (1 − evenness). *Measurable: Hill ^qD.*
- **Network science:** Λ = eigenvector-centrality Gini / distance-to-percolation. *Measurable: centrality dispersion.*
- **Stat-mech:** Λ = external field h (or susceptibility χ) pushing the max-entropy uniform off-equilibrium. *Measurable: χ.*
- **Localization:** Λ = distance to a mobility edge / multifractal-spectrum width. *Measurable: IPR, f(α).*
- **Mechanism design:** Λ = price-of-anarchy / Banzhaf-power dispersion vs equal. *Measurable: efficiency gap.*

**Test-first standouts** (most measurable + closest to the concept + one real physics realization):
**Gini/Lorenz, Buchert Q_D, N_e, softmax-T, KL** — all summarize "deviation of the influence-curve from
uniform" and all are computable. They JOIN the family; the crux (invariant iv) is unchanged — is the
deviation dynamically FORCED or dialed — now testable across many measurable proxies at once.

## Boundary
Discovery / Lane-2 instrument. Concepts are *held*, not banked; formalizations are tested; only
invariant-level falsification closes a concept. This prevents losing an idea to a single articulation.
