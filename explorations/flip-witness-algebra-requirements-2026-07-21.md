# The flip-witness algebra — requirements spec

Wave-2 collapsed four flagship problems (genuine becoming, the DE-amplitude VALUE, measurement's
Born + single-outcome legs, the low-entropy selection) into **one object**: a source-forced,
non-additive count that **grows the observable/record ALGEBRA**. This file is the acceptance rubric
for that object — the "issuing algebra." Each requirement is stated with the absorber it must beat,
because every requirement is exactly where a weaker candidate (the mirror-sector condensate) was
absorbed.

## The object
A dynamical process — the B5 source action's flow across the roll `N=e^{4p}` — that produces an
observable/record algebra which genuinely **grows**, is **authored by the source**, is
**productive** (not one-shot), and carries a **sourced non-additive count**.

## Requirements (necessary conditions; each names the absorber it defeats)

**R1 — Genuine algebra growth (W1), or an authored predicate (W2); NOT a state/representation change.**
- *W1 form:* the late-roll observable algebra is **non-isomorphic** to the early-roll one
  (`A_late ≇ A_early`).
- *W2 form:* a **new admissibility predicate authored by the source dynamics** — not a pre-existing
  kinematic predicate the process merely comes to satisfy.
- *Defeats:* the fixed-H **representation-change** route. A unitarily-inequivalent vacuum (e.g. the
  BCS condensed vacuum) is a new *state* on a **fixed** C\*-algebra — a fixed algebra hosts all
  phases as states. That is disclosure, not growth. (This is exactly how the condensate's best card
  was absorbed.)

**R2 — Mode-ISSUANCE, not mode-reshuffling.**
- Concretely in GU: the flow must **grow `ker(Γ)`** — issue new null pairs / new physical mode
  content — as `p` rolls, *gaining generators*.
- *Defeats:* the **Bogoliubov / fixed-CAR** absorber. Bogoliubov/BdG and ordinary SSB are
  automorphisms of a **fixed** generator set — they permute modes, they don't add them. This is the
  static-vs-dynamic crux: reshuffling a fixed mode set can cross a threshold *at most once*; issuing
  modes is what genuine, ongoing novelty requires.

**R3 — Productive / non-absorbing (unbounded novelty), not one-shot.**
- The growth must recur without a monotone cap: the count of genuine-novelty events must **not be
  finite-type-bounded** (effective type-count `Θ_eff` not finite).
- *Defeats:* the **one-shot / compact-image absorption** trap. The condensate's admissibility
  genesis (`F: empty→nonempty`) is monotone-absorbing — it fires once (`0→1`) and caps, so a fixed
  finite-type `A_∞` absorbs the whole roll → FTS/disclosure. A single genesis is still bookkeeping.

**R4 — Source-INTERNAL selection; self-generating (Gödelian); no external bit, no completed oracle.**
- The growth *and its direction* must be authored by the B5 source action itself.
- *Defeats:* two routes at once. (a) The **external-selector** route: the good/pathological branch
  cannot be picked by an external bit like the operative-C Krein sign (W211 proved it
  Gödel-independent of GU) — that buys "not-computable" at the cost of "externally contingent," the
  opposite of source-forced. (b) The **source-forcing disjunction**: it must not factor through
  either a posited fixed action (`fixed_source`) or the unbuilt action treated as a completed
  history/oracle (`completed_history`). Both horns kill source-forcing.

**R5 — A recordable witness, and `d>0` (real transduction).**
- The event must leave a **recordable trace/signature** (an N-curvature signature or a growth
  witness), so the Gödelian regime is *evidenced for the specific construction*, not merely
  non-computable-in-principle.
- `N` must be a genuine transduction of the source (`d>0`), **not** the source re-labeled (`d=0`).
- *Defeats:* the **type-distance / "count of source activity"** trap (a `d=0` re-label makes the
  accretion trivial).

**R6 — A non-additive count with a SOURCED coefficient (this is what buys the DE VALUE).**
- The grown count must be **non-additive** (coherent — the BCS `E_cond` already is), AND its scale
  coefficient must be **sourced from the dynamics** (e.g. tie the fluctuation scale `Θ` to `D`'s
  spectral gap), so `Λ~1/√N` carries a *derived* coefficient, not an imported one.
- *Defeats:* the **import** verdict of both wave-1 amplitude routes, where the `1/√N` **exponent**
  is native/consilient but the **value** is imported. R6 is what turns the exponent-consilience into
  a VALUE.

**R7 — Compatible with the supplied external bits (do NOT re-derive σ).**
- The Krein/DE **sign** stays external (σ). The algebra supplies **magnitude + becoming**, not the
  sign.
- *Rationale:* the Krein sign-selection no-go is class-relative and already accepted; spending the
  construction on deriving the sign is wasted effort. Respect it, don't fight it.

## Meta-requirement (the hostability tension — the deepest one)
**R2 (generator growth) is exactly what GU's fixed `Cl(9,5)=M(64,H)` substrate forbids** — `ker(Γ)`
is a fixed 96 null pairs of a fixed Clifford algebra. So the issuing algebra requires **either** an
*extension of GU* that permits generator/module growth (a source that enlarges the Clifford module),
**or** a *non-GU substrate* that natively issues modes (Lane 2 admits non-GU frames). This is the
current frontier tension: the growth the flagship needs vs. the static substrate that forbids it —
and the rigorous statement of *why the program cannot come from GU alone.*

## Relation to the P2C 9-item source-action spec
R1–R7 are the DU-specific refinement of P2C's source-action obligations (boundary DOF + law,
well-posedness, **return arrow**, **forward arrow**, resource ledger, BV/BRST closure, target-free,
falsifier). Specifically, they nail down what the **forward arrow** must mean at the algebra level:
issuing genuinely new content (R1/R2), productively (R3), from the source (R4), with a witness (R5),
carrying a sourced non-additive count (R6).
