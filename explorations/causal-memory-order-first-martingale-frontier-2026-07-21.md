## Answer up front

The wild swing found a **real escape** and then a **deeper kill**.

The real escape is that the local COV-02 problem was not universal. If `N(x)` is
the actual count of events in an event's causal past, there is no independent
local `delta N` sound mode. Its response is a retarded light-cone integral. A
closely related bi-wave memory has the metric light cone exactly. Thus the old
`c_N^2=3 Omega_V>1` result does **not** rule out this class.

The deeper kill is that the raw causal past has too much scale symmetry. In
every power-law universe its four-volume is exactly `C(p)t^4`; therefore
`1/sqrt(N)` is exactly proportional to `t^-2`, just like the total cosmic
density. The proposed component is consequently always present at a fixed
fraction. If it is quiet enough during radiation to preserve a conventional
early universe, it is negligible late. If it is large enough late, it dominates
the early universe. If it is conserved, its pressure copies radiation and matter
and it never becomes dark energy at all.

The most heterodox version — a signed action martingale on an order-first causal
set — does make the half-power and sign arise statistically. It also produces a
new exact spatial prediction: two events' `Lambda` values are correlated by the
fraction of their causal pasts that overlap. But the unwindowed process is much
too noisy early, rarely keeps the same positive sign for a long late interval,
and decays rather than approaching constant `Lambda` in de Sitter.

So nonlocality alone is not the missing ingredient. The surviving frontier is a
source-owned order-first growth law that **generates a dimensional memory scale**
and a causal response/noise action before cosmological fitting.
