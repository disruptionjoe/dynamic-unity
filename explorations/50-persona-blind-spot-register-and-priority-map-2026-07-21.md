The source panel is preserved under stable IDs `P01-A` through `P50-B`:

- `A` is that persona's most-important suggestion; `B` is its second-most-important suggestion.
- A `★` marks panel-level high conviction. It may be lowered only by new evidence, not by
  deduplication or convenience.
- Priority tiers mean: **P0** = resolve before the affected flagship claim is treated as physically
  load-bearing or banked; **P1** = highest-leverage active research/hardening; **P2** = important
  extension or enabling work; **P3** = useful background work.

  close all of its members; each member receives its own evidence-backed disposition.

### Progression protocol

- Every `Pxx-A/B` item begins **OPEN**. Valid later dispositions are `ACTIVE`, `SATISFIED`,
  `REFUTED`, `SUPERSEDED`, `DEFERRED-WITH-RECHECK`, or `ROUTED`; deletion is not a disposition.
- Automated progression selects the highest-priority eligible OPEN item or coherent work package
  that does not collide with a recent active run. Every selected swing cites the source item IDs.
- A single result may address several IDs, but closeout records a separate disposition and evidence
  pointer for each one. Shared work never silently flattens minority/high-conviction suggestions.
- P0 items outrank coefficient refinement, analogy expansion and paper seeding unless Joe directs a
  different swing. P1/P2 work remains preserved and eligible rather than being discarded by ranking.
- Dispositions append to the ledger below; the original wording of the source item is immutable.

### Why this ordering

WP-01 comes first because it can invalidate the current interpretation of R6b's “positive” in one
bounded swing: a stable `Ω_Λ` tracker that neither conserves covariantly nor accelerates is not a
dark-energy solution, regardless of how its coefficient is sourced. WP-02 comes next because the
North-Star sentence currently asks several unconstructed `N` identities to carry one another.
WP-06 is coequal in epistemic importance: until banking and evidence independence are coherent, even
a correct physics result cannot be promoted reliably. WP-03 and WP-04 are the constructive recovery
program once those three immediate validity questions are explicit.
